Why Capture & Co.

Built by photographers, for photographers

Capture & Co. started in a small shop lined with secondhand Nikons and rolls of expired film, and grew because photographers kept walking back in with questions no online store could answer. Today we stock mirrorless bodies, prime lenses, tripods, and darkroom essentials for everyone from first-time shooters in Bengaluru to wedding photographers working weekends across Rajasthan. Every camera that leaves our warehouse has been checked by hand, not just scanned off a shelf. We ship pan-India with cash on delivery and easy EMI options, because good glass shouldn't wait on a metro city pin code. Our team answers gear questions in Hindi, English, and half a dozen other languages, because photography advice loses something in translation. GST invoices are generated automatically for every order, and our trade-in desk gives fair credit on your old body toward something new. Whether you're chasing monsoon light in Kerala or shooting street portraits in Old Delhi, we build our catalogue around how India actually shoots — not how a catalogue from abroad assumes it does.
